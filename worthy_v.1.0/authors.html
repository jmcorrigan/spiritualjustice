About the Authors

Scott Brown

I'm a senior studying journalism and environmental science at the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL. I'm from both Philadelphia and New Jersey.

My passion is working for social injustice, both as a journalist and an organizer. I use video, writing and any other medium that can give a platform to those fighting for change. I also work with students to build campaigns around issues like higher education reform, environmental justice and racial equality.


Jack Corrigan

I'm a senior at Northwestern University studying journalism and economics.

I read the news obsessively, geek out over data, and love politics, spicy food and blues rock.

This summer, I was based in Santa Barbara, CA, writing about politics and business for Graphiq, Inc. I've previously worked in Washington, D.C., as a national security and health reporter for the Medill News Service, and in Chicago as a web production intern for WGN-TV.
